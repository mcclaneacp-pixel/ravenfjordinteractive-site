This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and shared when you use the mobile game Neon Burn.

Information Collected by Ravenfjord Interactive

Neon Burn does not require users to create an account, log in, use chat, participate in multiplayer features, upload content, or provide their name, email address, or other directly identifying information to Ravenfjord Interactive.

Advertising

Neon Burn may use Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner ads and rewarded advertisements.

If Google AdMob is enabled, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect or process information such as:

  • IP address and approximate general location
  • Device and advertising identifiers
  • App launches, taps, video views, and ad interactions
  • Diagnostic, performance, and crash-related information

This information may be used for advertising, analytics, ad measurement, fraud prevention, security, and improving advertising services.

Google may use information to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements, depending on the user's location, age, device settings, consent choices, and applicable law.

For more information about how Google handles information, review the Google Privacy Policy .

Rewarded Advertisements

Neon Burn may allow users to voluntarily watch a rewarded advertisement in exchange for an in-game benefit, such as a temporary shield. Watching a rewarded advertisement is optional.

Consent and Privacy Choices

Where required by law, Neon Burn may request consent before loading personalized advertising or collecting certain advertising-related information.

Users may also be able to manage advertising preferences through their device settings, Google account settings, or any privacy controls presented inside the game.

Local Device Storage

Neon Burn may store limited gameplay information locally on the user's device, including settings, selected controls, unlocked content, and high scores.

This locally stored information is used to maintain game progress and preferences and is not used to identify the user personally.

Children's Privacy

Neon Burn is not intended for children under the minimum age permitted by applicable law. Ravenfjord Interactive does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect information associated with the game. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when Neon Burn's features, advertising services, or data practices change. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
